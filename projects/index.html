Coursework - Experience - Projects - Involvement

Epidemia | Stochastic Modeling Summer 2020
This summer, I am working with a project partner, Sneha Sridhar, on a model stochastic model to predict the behavior of COVID-19. We will use the model as a back-end algorithm for important applications such as predicting a second wave, and computing a safety index for travel routes by using the model in conjunction with the Google Maps API.

Rias | Cellular Automata Spring 2019
Last fall, I started working with a project partner, Alec Zhou, on a model that is inspired from Conway's Game of Life, which observes the evolution of cellular automata on an arbritary set of rules. We are experimenting with a variety of boundary conditions and rules in an attempt to find interesting patterns and behaviors that may have implications in decision automation and self-replicating machines.
