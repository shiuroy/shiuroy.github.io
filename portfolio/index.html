Srujay Korlakunta // Portfolio

Ford UAV App (design) 2019
I led a team to help engineers at Ford track and manage their UAV flights. We designed & built an app and a Ford vehicle interface that log trips and map UAV restricted flight zones using FAA data. This was done as a contract for Ford as a part of Mobile Developers of Berkeley.

Techfair @ Berkeley (website) 2019
Techfair is UC Berkeley's all-new showcase for student makers. It's focused on bringing together Berkeley's brightest student makers and connecting them to users, mentors, and investors. I worked on marketing for Techfair, which involved designing graphics for our Facebook and Twitter pages, and designing our website at techfair.dev.

Streamline (code) (design) 2017
Streamline is a social music experience on iOS and Android. Share your niche or basic taste in music with your friends by posting to a live feed. With a team of 7, we designed and developed this app for both iOS and Android.

Coppell Student Media for iOS (app) 2015
Back in high school, my friends and I built an app for iOS that lets students, parents, and teachers get access to our school's student newspaper. We wrote it in Objective-C back in 2015, and I later led a complete re-write in React Native.
